About Me

Hi, I’m Manas Tripathi—a CSE student at BITS Pilani, Hyderabad, passionate about building impactful tech. I love creating software that solves real problems, whether that means writing clean code, training machine learning models, or designing intuitive user experiences. I’ve worked with tools like Python, React, TensorFlow, and scikit-learn, and enjoy learning by building. On campus, I’m the Design Head and a core team member of the Computer Science Association, and I’ve also been closely involved with E-Cell, working on venture support and ecosystem-building. One of my favorite projects was Ace-It, an AI-powered mock interview platform that made it to the finals of a student entrepreneurship program. Outside of tech, you’ll usually find me reading about startups or playing football.

Let’s connect—whether it’s for an opportunity, a collaboration, or a great conversation about tech, products, and ideas that matter.

Projects

2D Home Floor Planner – Swing-based GUI Application

Developed a comprehensive Java Swing-based 2D floor planning application with canvas and control panels to design customizable house layouts by adding and positioning rooms of various types, placing doors, windows, and furniture, enforcing non-overlapping constraints, and enabling features like drag, rotate, save, and load for an interactive design experience.

Database Design and Implementation for Nova Pharmacy Chain Management

Designed and implemented a relational database system for Nova Pharmacy Chain to manage data related to patients, doctors, pharmaceutical companies, pharmacies, drugs, and prescriptions. Focused on ER/EER modeling, relational schema design, and SQL/PL-SQL implementation for data entry, updates, and report generation.

So far I’ve contributed to real-world projects and impactful student-led initiatives—like Climetaverse, Ace-It, CSA, and E-Cell.
